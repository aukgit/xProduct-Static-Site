What does it mean?

x-Product is a concept and a name that we couldn’t figure out at time of development. The name X represents that it can be transformed into any product. Thus, the name X seems to be perfect as it can vary over the requirements and needs.

What is the product?

x-Product will contain xbusiness-engines like WordPress template which will be well-suited for each specific business and additional changes can be done as additional layers over that template.

What else?

Moreover, x-Product will have it’s own compiler which can communicate with Node JS, C# and probably Java. (hint. if you are good at string processing then you are a good candidate for this job)

x-Product in Details

x-Product is a platform for both programmer and non-programmer where they can convert their business idea into a working business engine. People who has the knowledge of JavaScript, C# or Java they can develope their engine by using any of the languages. But those who does not have programming language knowledge they can use our GUI Library to built their app. After that they can compile their app into any of the mentioned languages.

Experinece developers can develope customizable business engine by using our platform and sell them via our marketplace. All the engine will be tested and verified by our team.
